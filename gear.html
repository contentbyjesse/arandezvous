The 35L Rule

One bag. Carry-on only. Domestic or international, three days or three weeks, this is the constraint that makes every trip better. You move faster, you think less about stuff, and you're never waiting at baggage claim.

35L

Max bag volume, always

  • Everything must fit in a personal item or small carry-on.
  • If something doesn't earn its spot twice, it gets cut next trip.
  • Merino wool wherever possible. Wears multiple days.
  • Running hot means fewer layers, lighter fabrics, no fleece.
  • The camera sling counts as your personal item.
